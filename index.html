Our story

A few chapters from our journey...

Home Coming 2018

With a little courage and a lot of nerves, Seann asked Jayleen to Homecoming. That night marked their first date—and the beginning of a love story that would last far beyond high school.

High School Years

We spent our high school years side by side in band and indoor drumline, growing together through long rehearsals, competitions, and leadership roles that shaped who we are today.

Welcoming Storm

In 2023, our family grew by four paws when we welcomed our boy, Storm. Our playful white husky has filled our home with even more love, laughter, and adventure.

University Years

As high school sweethearts, our story began long before college. We were incredibly blessed to continue that journey side by side at UT Austin, where we shared four unforgettable years together and proudly graduated from UT Austin in the Spring of 2024.

The Proposal

After closing on our first home and finishing the long day of moving in, we finally had a quiet moment alone. In the stillness of our new home, Seann got down on one knee and asked Jayleen to spend forever with him.
